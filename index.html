Sharing Your Calendar

To share your calendar with Karen:

  1. Open outlook and go to your calender
  2. On the Home tab click the Calendar Permissions button. A new window will open.
  3. Press the Add... button.
  4. Type "Alarid" in the search box, choose "Alarid, Karen", then press the Add button.
  5. Press OK
  6. Choose "Alarid, Karen" from the list of names
  7. Choose "Free/Busy time, subject, location" from the Read section
  8. Choose "Folder visible" from the Other section
  9. Press the OK button.
